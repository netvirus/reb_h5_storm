Tetrarch Kaitar:
*Gran Kain* -- Sovereign of Destruction, Father ofDarkness! If the sunlight that pierces our eyes was made by Einhasad,then the darkness that swallows the light was made by the Dark Lord,Gran Kain. Other races call Gran Kain a devil, fear him and exclude hisfollowers. However, as the late afternoon shadow slowly overtakes theland, the power of Gran Kain will surpass all other gods -- and when thelight is put out, flames extinguished, and the wind dies, the groundwill part and all rivers and oceans will become dry. Then, only darknesswill remain.

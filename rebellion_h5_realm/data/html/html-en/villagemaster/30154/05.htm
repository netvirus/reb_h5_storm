Hierarch Asterios:
*Elven Wizards* enjoy a deep connection to thespirits of nature, and practice more powerful and complicated magic thanElven Mystics. These arts include elemental magic, black magic andsummoning magic. They command fire and wind to attack enemies, takeopponents' lives with black magic, and summon the spirits of mothernature as servitors. Among Elven Wizards, those who want to studyelemental magic become *Spellsingers*, and those who want to reach theultimate heights of summoning magic become *Elemental Summoners*.

Hierarch Asterios:
The *Elven Oracle* is the Elven Mystic who controlsspirits of light, life, innocence and purification, derived from thedivinity of Einhasad and Eva. These priests give spiritual advice toother Elves. They practice the arts of white magic and supplementarymagic, which offer powerful means of protecting and healing themselvesand their fellows, rather than attacking enemies. Among Elven Oracles,those who are spiritually gifted could become *Elven Elders*. ElvenElders are the most advanced priests to receive the blessings ofEinhasad and Eva. They practice the most powerful white magic.

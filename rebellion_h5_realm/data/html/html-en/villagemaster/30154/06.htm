Hierarch Asterios:
To become an Elven Wizard, your level must be atleast 20 and you must pass the Test of the Elven Wizard. The Elf*Rosella* is in charge of testing young Elves who aspire to be ElvenWizards. If you want to know more about the test, go to her. Remember,even if you earn the qualifications, we cannot change your occupationhere. That is because we wish for young Elves to go out beyond theforest, to experience Human society and understand the larger world. Therefore, if you wish to change occupations, you must leave the ElvenForest and go to the Human kingdom of Aden. The nearest place for achange of profession is *Gludio*, which is located in the south of theforest. If you follow the road and cross the Neutral Zone, you willcross a bridge and come to a *Gludio Castle Town. * Look for *High PriestRaymond* at the temple. He will change your occupation, if you areready.

Hierarch Asterios:
To become an Elven Scout, your level must be atleast 20 and you must pass the Test of the Elven Scout. Remember, evenif you earn the qualifications we cannot change your profession here. That is because it is our wish for young Elves to go out beyond theforest, to experience Human society and understand the larger world. Therefore, if you wish to change profession, you must leave the ElvenForest and go to the Human kingdom of Aden. The nearest place for achange of profession is *Gludio*, which is located in the south of theforest. If you follow the road and cross the neutral zone, you willcross a bridge and come to a town -- the *town of Gludio*. Find *GrandMaster Rains* of the fighter guild there. He will change your professionwhen you are ready.

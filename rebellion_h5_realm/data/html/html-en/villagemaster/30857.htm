High Priest Orven:
Welcome to the Town of Aden, the heart of thekingdom. People speak of this place as the kingdom's cultural andreligious center. If this is true, then this sanctuary of life is trulythe apex of faith that all who serve Einhasad must preserve. But trueholiness may also be found in the ordinary, the everyday, the mundane. The prayer of a commoner may resonate more deeply with the mother oflight than even the prayer of a high priest, so perhaps this place isnot so different from other temples.

To be a Dark Avenger, you must bring 3 Tokens. The *Mark of Duty* isonly given to those who pass the Trial of Duty. You must be above *level35* to take the test. To take the Trial of Duty, visit *Grand MasterHannavalt of Giran Castle Town*. The *Mark of Trust* is only given tothose who pass the Testimony of Trust. You must be above *level 37* totake the test. To take the Testimony of Trust, visit *High PriestHollint at the Town of Oren*. And finally the *Mark of Witchcraft* isonly given to those who pass the Test of Witchcraft. You must be above*level 39* to take the test. To take the Test of Witchcraft, go to the*Corridor of Death and visit Orim of the Shadow*.

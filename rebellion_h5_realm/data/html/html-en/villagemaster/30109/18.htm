And to take the Trial of theSeeker, visit *Master Dufner of Giran Castle Town*. The *Mark of Trust*is only given to those who pass the Testimony of Trust. You must beabove *level 37* to take the test. To take the Testimony of Trust, visit*High Priest Hollint at the Town of Oren*. And finally, the *Mark of theSearcher* is only given to those who pass the Test of the Searcher. Youmust be above *level 39* to take the test. To take the Test of theSearcher, visit *Master Luther at the Hunters' Village*.

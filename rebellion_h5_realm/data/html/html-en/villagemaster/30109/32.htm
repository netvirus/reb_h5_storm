To be a Gladiator, you must bring 3 Tokens. The *Mark of theChallenger* is only given to those who pass the Trial of the Challenger. You must be above *level 35* to take the test. And to take the Trial ofthe Challenger, visit *Kash of Dion Castle Town*. The *Mark of Trust* isonly given to those who pass the Testimony of Trust. You must be above*level 37* to take the test. To take the Testimony of Trust, visit *HighPriest Hollint at the Town of Oren*. And finally the *Mark of theDuelist* is only given to those who pass the Test of the Duelist. Youmust be above *level 39* to take the test. To take the Test of theDuelist, meet *Duelist Kaien of the Town of Oren*.

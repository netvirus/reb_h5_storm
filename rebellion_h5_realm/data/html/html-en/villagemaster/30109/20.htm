A Hawkeye is a specialized occupation of a 'Fighter with a Bow' versionof a Rogue. So the role he plays in combat and during party play isbasically a 'Rogue with a Bow. ' Hawkeyes true skills shine during SiegeBattle. It is dreadful to imagine a number of Hawkeyes shooting at once,at an opponent at a far distance. They have basic skills such asstrengthening their bows and various other skills such as shootingseveral arrows simultaneously. To be a Hawkeye, your level must be over40 and you need to collect the Mark of the Seeker, the Mark of Trust,and the Mark of Sagittarius.

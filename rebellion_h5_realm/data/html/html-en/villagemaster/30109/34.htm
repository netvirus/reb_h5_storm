Warlords specialize in polearms or pole-type weapons. He is weaker thana Gladiator during 1 on 1 combat, but can give great damages fightingseveral opponents at once. So he is advantageous during battle against anumber of opponents. He shines during Siege Battle. He uses skills whichuse range attack with polearms or pole-type weapons. To be a Warlord,your level must be over 40 and you need to collect the Mark of theChallenger, the Mark of Trust, and the Mark of the Champion.

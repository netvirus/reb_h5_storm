You must pass individual tests to obtain marks. The *Mark of Duty* isonly given to those who pass the Trial of Duty. You must be above *level35* to take the test. To take the Trial of Duty, visit *Grand MasterHannavalt of Giran Castle Town*. The *Mark of Life* is only given tothose who pass the Testimony of Life. You must be above *level 37* totake the test. To take the Test of Life, visit *Master Cardien of DionCastle Town*. And finally the *Mark of the Healer* is only given tothose who pass the Test of the Healer. You must be above *level 39* totake the test. To take the Test of the Healer, visit *Priest Bandellosat the temple of Giran Castle Town*.

The Plainswalker is almost similar to the Human Treasure Hunter. However DEX is very important for this occupation, so it can be saidthat their basic abilities are better compared to Treasure Hunters. Also, the Plainswalker have certain qualities that other occupationshave which help its party. He acts as an Assassin during Siege Battle. He greatly damages opponents with sudden blows. He has different skillsthat other occupations do not have. He can lure an enemy from its raceand he can act dead during emergencies. To be a Plainswalker, your levelmust be over 40 and you need to collect the Mark of the Seeker, the Markof Life, and the Mark of the Searcher.

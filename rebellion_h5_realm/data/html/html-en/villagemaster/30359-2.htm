Tetrarch Kaitar:
Shilen, Mother of the Abyss, is the Goddess whocontrols fate and death. Shilen permits all life to be limited, and itis she who enables death, so that all living things can return to theiroriginal state of nothingness. Other races do not understand the eternalcycle of birth and death. They only see one side of death, and fear andhate Shilen. Because of these prejudices, in the Human Kingdom, the markof Shilen hanging around one's neck will lead to being burnt at thestake for heresy. In spite of this persecution, there are secret groupsof Humans who follow the goddess Shilen. In a sense, their faith is thesame as the Dark Elves. If only we could know who they were.

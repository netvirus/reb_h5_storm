Grand Master Bitz:
The *Rogue* is the most unique among the threeprofessions. They choose agility over the Warrior's power, and profitover the Human Knight's honor. They are armed with small daggers andbows and arrows, and can scout enemy camps without making any sound. Their abilities can make a great difference in battle. Rogues can laterchange to higher-level professions, like the *Hawkeye*, who specializesin archery, or the *Treasure Hunter*, whose primary weapon is thedagger.

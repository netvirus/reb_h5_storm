High Priestess Levian:
The *Elven Oracle* is the Elven Mystic whocontrols spirits of light, life, innocence and purification, derivedfrom the divinity of Einhasad and Eva. These priests give spiritualadvice to other Elves. They practice the arts of white magic andsupplementary magic, which offer powerful means of protecting andhealing themselves and their fellows, rather than attacking enemies. Among Elven Oracles, those who are spiritually gifted could become*Elven Elders*. Elven Elders are the most advanced priests to receivethe blessings of Einhasad and Eva. They practice the most powerful whitemagic. But first, to become an Elven Oracle, you must pass the Test ofthe Elven Oracle and bring back the *leaf of the Oracle* as proof ofyour accomplishment.

High Priestess Levian:
You need the appropriate level and token tobecome an Elven Oracle. To change profession, your level must be atleast 20, and to become an Oracle, you must have the token that provesthat you passed the Test of the Elven Oracle. However, it seems yourlevel is lower than 20, and you have not yet passed the Test of theElven Oracle. The first thing you need to do is train harder to reachlevel 20. Then you must pass the test and bring back the *leaf of theOracle* as proof. To take the test, go to *Priest Manuel, who can befound in the sanctuary of the town of Gludio*. To get to the town ofGludio, follow the road to the east. You must pass his test and bringback the *leaf of the Oracle*.

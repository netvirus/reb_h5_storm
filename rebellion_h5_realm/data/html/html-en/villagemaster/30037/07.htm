High Priestess Levian:
The *leaf of the Oracle* is a token given onlyto Elves who have the qualifications to become an Elven Oracle. *PriestManuel*, who has taught Elven Oracles for a long time, is in charge ofthe Test of the Elven Oracle. He can be found in the temple in the townof Gludio. If you follow the road that runs east of the village, youwill come to the town of Gludio. The temple is in the northern part ofthe village. Go to the sanctuary there to find Priest Manuel.

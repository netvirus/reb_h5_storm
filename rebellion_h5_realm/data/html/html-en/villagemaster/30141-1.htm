Tetrarch Talloth:

He also holds the ultimate magical power, due toa contract with Drevanul. Because of this, the morale of our forceswas broken, and just as we were about to win the war, we had no choicebut to retreat. Our Patriarch was a political leader, but also thecentral source of the black magic used by our race. Losing that powermeant that our magic was greatly weakend.

Head Blacksmith Ferris:
There used to be plenty of work for usblacksmiths here in Aden Town, especially with war with Elmore always onthe horizon. Tapoy of the Black Anvil Guild did very well for himself inthose days, I can tell you. I thought that when those wearisome warswere finally over I would have time enough for my other interests, butwe seem busier than ever these days.

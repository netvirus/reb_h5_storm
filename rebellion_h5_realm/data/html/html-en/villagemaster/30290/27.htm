Grand Master Xenos:
To become a Shillien Oracle, the appropriate leveland token are required. Your level must be at least 20 to qualify tochange profession. Also, a token that proves that you passed the Test ofthe Shillien Oracle is required. However, it seems your level is nothigh enough, and you have not yet passed the Test of the ShillienOracle. First, try to raise your level to 20 or higher. To take thetest, you should visit *Sorceress Sidra* of the Dark Elven Guild in the*town of Gludio*. You can change profession to Shillien Oracle only ifyou pass her test and bring back the *orb of abyss* as a token. You canget to the town of Gludio by following the road towards the east.

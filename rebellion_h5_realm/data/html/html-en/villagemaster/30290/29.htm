To become a Shillien Oracle, the appropriate leveland token are required. Your level must be at least 20 to qualify tochange profession. Also, a token that proves that you passed the Test ofthe Shillien Oracle is required. I am satisfied with your level, but itseems that you have not passed the Test of the Shillien Oracle. To takethe test, you should find *Sorceress Sidra* in the Dark Elven Guild ofthe *town of Gludio*. You can change profession to Shillien Oracle onlyif you pass her test and bring back the *orb of abyss* as a token. Youcan get to the town of Gludio by following the road towards the east.

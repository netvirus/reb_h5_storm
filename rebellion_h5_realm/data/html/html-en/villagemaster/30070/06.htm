High Priest Sylvain:
The *Elven Oracle* is an Elven Mystic who controlsspirits of light, life, innocence and purification, derived from thedivinity of Einhasad and Eva. These priests give spiritual advice toother Elves. They practice the arts of white magic and supplementarymagic, which offer powerful means of protecting and healing themselvesand their fellows, rather than attacking enemies. Among Oracles, thosewho are spiritually gifted could become *Elven Elders*. Elders are themost advanced priests to receive the blessings of Einhasad and Eva. Theypractice the most powerful white magic. But first, to become an Oracle,you must pass the Test of the Elven Oracle and bring back the *leaf ofthe Oracle* as proof of your accomplishment.

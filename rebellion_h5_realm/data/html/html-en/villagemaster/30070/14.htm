To become a Cleric, one's faith must be verified. *Priest Zigaunt, in the village of Gludin*, may judge the purity of yourfaith. If you pass his test and return with the *mark of faith,* you canchange profession to become a Cleric. Priest Zigaunt is in the templeof the village of Gludin. You can reach the village of Gludin bycontinuing west on the road. The temple is in the northeast part of thevillage. Of course, using a Gatekeeper is much faster.

Tetrarch Thifiell:
*Palus Knights* are the elite knights of the DarkElves. Their excellent swordsmanship is based on swift, precise attacksand effective defense, and it cannot be compared to other races thatdepend on mere strength or coarser skills. The most talented of PalusKnights are selected for the highest order, as a *Shillien Knight*, orbecome *Bladedancers*, with almost unearthly skill in swordsmanship.

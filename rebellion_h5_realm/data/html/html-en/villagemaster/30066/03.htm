*Elven Knights* choose swordsmanship over archery. It is amazing to watch them fighting, because they use unique stylesthat emphasize defense as much as attack. Their polished techniquesconfuse their enemies and mesmerize those who are watching. These days,many Elven Knights enter the Human world in the service of Einhasad andEva, even though they were originally created to protect the MotherTrees from invaders. The most talented of Elven Knights are sometimesselected as *Temple Knights*, the highest order of the Elves, or*Swordsingers*, who train Elven swordsmanship to a fine art. Of course,much hard training, as well as great talent, is required. To become anElven Knight, you must pass the Test of the Elven Knight and bring backthe *Elven Knight brooch*. The Elven Knight brooch proves that you havethe qualifications to become an Elven Knight*.

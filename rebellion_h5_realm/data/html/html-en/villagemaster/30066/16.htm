The *Rogue* is a unique profession among the threechoices Human Fighters are offered. They choose agility over theWarrior's power, and profit over the Knight's honor. They prefer tocarry small daggers and bows and arrows, and scout enemy camps withouttheir footsteps making any sound. It would be a hundred times moredifficult to win battles without them. Their missions include scouting,investigating, spying and assassination, among others. Rogues with greatfame and reputation can change to the higher-level profession of the*Hawkeye*, who use bows and arrows, or the *Treasure Hunter*, who usesdaggers . Of course, it is very difficult to reach that level. Tobecome a Rogue, you must also bring a *letter of recommendation from theGuard Captain Bezique*. It is a token that shows you are qualified tobecome a Rogue.

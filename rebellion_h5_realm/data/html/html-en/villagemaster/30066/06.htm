Grand Master Pabris:
*Elven Scouts* are the sentries of the forest, andhave protected the Elven home from invaders for thousands of years. Since their footsteps are lighter than feathers, they can walk and runover the fallen leaves without making any noise as they approach theiropponents. They suddenly launch fatal attacks with bows or daggers, thendisappear as if into thin air as they blend into the forest. The mosttalented Elven Scouts are selected as *Plainswalker*, the Ranger elite. Others among the Elven Scouts are selected as *Silver Rangers*, andtrain in the utmost mastery of archery. Of course, much hard training,as well as great talent, is required. To become an Elven Scout, you mustpass the test and bring back Reisa's Recommendation as proof.

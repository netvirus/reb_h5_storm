Grand Master Pabris:
*Warriors* pursue extreme offensive power. Sinceattack power is very important for them, their skills emphasize offense. They also have far more HP than those of other professions. They oftentake the front line of battle with their great physical strength. Warriors who continuously train and build experience can attain thehigher-level profession of the *Warlord* or the *Gladiator*. To become aWarrior, you must pass the Test of the Warrior and bring back the*medallion of the Warrior* as proof.

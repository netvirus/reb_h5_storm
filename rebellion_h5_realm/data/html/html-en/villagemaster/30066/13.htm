Grand Master Pabris:
Unlike the Warrior, who focuses all energy on astrong attack, the *Human Knight* places more importance on the balancebetween attack and defense. Human Knights have great courage and must bewilling to sacrifice all for their companions. With shields andexcellent armor, they place themselves between monsters and theircompanions. They are the most faithful protectors of kings and lords. They belong to a very different order than mercenary soldiers, who workfor profit. Those who are recognized as great Knights may gain a stillhigher-level profession, such as the *Paladin* or the *Dark Avenger*. Tobecome a Human Knight, you must pass the Test of the Human Knight andbring back the *sword of ritual* as proof.

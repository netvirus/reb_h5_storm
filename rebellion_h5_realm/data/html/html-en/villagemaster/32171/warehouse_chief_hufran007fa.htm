Warehouse Chief Hoffa:

To meet the qualifications for Scavenger, you must have at least achieved level 20 and have passed the Scavenger test.
However, even those basic requirements aren't enough -- only those who understand the great world beyond the plateau are truyly qualified to complete a class transfer and become empowered to control the world's economy.
To take the test, speak with Collector Pippi of the Bronze Key Guild in the Dwarven Village. The Newbie Travel Token will help you.
She will tell you everything you need to know. Once you have met the qualifications, go to Gludin Village and meet Warehouse Chief Moke. If he deems you worthy, he will transfer you to Scavenger.

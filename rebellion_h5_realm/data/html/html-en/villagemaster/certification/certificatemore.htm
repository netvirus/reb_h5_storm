Subclass Skill Certification:
You can be certified at levels 65, 70, 75and 80. You can practice common skills at Level 65 and 70, common orspecial skills at Level 75, and ultimate skills at level 80. Commonskills are not occupation specific. They can be learned regardless ofclass. Special skills and ultimate skills are specific to youroccupation. The effects of skills learned through this certification canoverlap each other, even if they are the same kind of skill.

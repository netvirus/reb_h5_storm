Lighthouse Keeper Rocksewll:
Long ago, they say they sacrificed virginstothe sea, but times have changed and there isno sailor who keeps thistradition. Instead, weset afloat a boat that contains a doll in theshapeof a Human maiden. Now we have tomake that doll. I've asked WarehouseKeeper Rolfe, who ishandy with his hands, to make the doll, but westillhave to make the dolls long hair. For that, Ineed high quality fur yarn. Hunt the keltris on the island and gather aboutten bundles of fur, thentake them to Cristle inthe next village. You can find her at the wellinfront of the village warehouse. Give the fur toCristal, and she willspin it into yarn. Take theyarn and give it to Warehouse Keeper Rolfe. Hewill probably finish the doll on the spot. Assoon as the doll isfinished, bring it back to me. I will give you a generous reward for yourlabor.

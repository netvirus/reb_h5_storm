General Ermian:
We deployed on an exploration mission, one ship amongmany. In an unknown region we encountered something that, to this day, Ican't describe. A large. . mass. . that shifted colors and shape. Assoon as we came into contact with it our ships were pulled into somekind of powerful vortex. It swallowed ship and soldier alike. I stillhear their screams sometimes. No one knew what it was, but I knew whatmy gut said. My gut said to get the hell away from it, far away. It maybe the only reason any of us lived.
We don't know where we are or if there are any other survivors. So wehave setup camp here in hopes of finding our men. But with every minutewe wait, our chances of returning home diminish.

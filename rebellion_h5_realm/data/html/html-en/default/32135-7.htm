If you use your head while hunting, you should have about 60 SP by thetime you reach level 5, *Master Pintage* or Master Gwinter can tell youthe skill in which you can train. Skills are divided into active andpassive skills. Active skills require that you intentionally use them,while passive skills increase your basic abilities from the moment youlearn them. When you reach level 5, you'll want to learn activeoffensive skills first, Power strike, Mortal Blow, and Power Shot areyour choices. Power Strike is for those who fight with a sword, MortalBlow for dagger fighters, and Power Shot for those who use the bow. Power Shot deals a great deal of damage, but also consumes a greatquantity of MP, so it has to be used wisely Make sure you choose theskill you really want.

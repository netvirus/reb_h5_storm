Priestess Restina:
The Lady of the Lake is Eva, the mother of allElves. Even before the Elven migration, the people of Innadril knew thegoddess of water well. There was a time when Elves regarded Eva asunapproachable and sacred, and this is still true in most parts of theElven Forest. But due to the influence of the indigenous religion ofthis area, Eva is a much more accessible and personal goddess to us.

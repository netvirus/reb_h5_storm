Dilios:
Currently, the Mounted Troop controls the Seed of Destruction. You must go there and defeat its captain, Tiat. A command channelconsisting of 5 parties should be established in order to attack theSeed. Then entry is possible through Sergeant Allenos, who has beendispatched there. There is a time limit for attacking the Seed ofDestruction, however. If you cannot carry out an assault within 2 hours,you will be expelled. The interior of the Seed is blocked by variousobstacles, among them a powerful barrier protected by 3 devices. Onceyou have broken through all the barriers and gates, you will reach theFortress of Destruction.

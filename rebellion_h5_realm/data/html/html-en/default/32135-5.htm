You can begin to learn skills when you reach level 5. You will receiveinstruction in new skills from the Masters of the Warriors Guild. Theguildhall lies to the east, near the tower at the crossroads in thecenter of the village. Now, pay attention; you'll need *SP* to gain yourskills, SP is short for skill ponits, and they're earned along withexperience whenever you defeat a monster, SP are deducted whenever youlearn a new skill. You can check your SP by clicking the CharacterStatus button, then on the Status tab in the window. Of course, thenumber of SP you need will vary, depending on the skill you're after.

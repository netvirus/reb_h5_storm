If you have powerful companions, they may purchase equipment for you. Otherwise, equipment for novices is provided by the Temple of Shilen. For now, you should seek to master the arms and armour you alreadypossess. You will sometimes obtain arms and armor from the monsters youhunt. With some luck, you may acquire *cloth shoes, apprentice's shoes,or a* *cloth cap. * The temple will provide you with two differentblades, a * dagger* and a squire's sword. The dagger is swift, but itsaccuracy suffers, while the squire's sword (one-handed sword) isnoticeably slower than the dagger, but strikes with greater accuracy. Remember that different weapons may call upon different skills in theiruse. Return to me when you have achieved *level 6. * and I will teach youmore about weapons.

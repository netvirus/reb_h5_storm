When you reach level 5, you should pick one of two weapon skills,depending on your preference in weapons. If you fight with a dagger,learn *Mortal Blow* , and if you wield a sword learn Power Strike. Whenyou have learned the weapon skill of your choice, use any remaining SPto acquire some passive skills. Also, if you are using a bow, *PowerShot* is an invaluable skill. It is a powerful offensive skill thatallows you to defeat even monsters who are somewhat stronger than you.

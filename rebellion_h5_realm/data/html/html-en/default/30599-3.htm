You should have been given two weapons -- a *dagger* and a *Squire'sSword*. Perhaps you have already noticed that daggers and swords performdifferently in battle. The dagger is swift, but its accuracy suffers,while the squire's sword is noticeably slower than the dagger, butstrikes with greater accuracy. Remember that different weapons may callupon different skills in their use. Either is a worthy weapon; it is upto you to decide which suits you best. When you have saved around 500adena, buy a *Bone Dagger* if that's what you prefer, or a *short sword*if swords are more to your taste. You may be able to improve your armourfrom the spoils of te monsters you slay, but that is a matter of luck. If you reach level 5 and are still lacking gloves, shoes or a helmet,you should consider purchasing the cheapest ones offered by the store.

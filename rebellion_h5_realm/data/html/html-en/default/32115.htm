Asamah:
I am Asamah, son of Mushika, chief of the Elroki tribe. Theseare our lands. In them lies perhaps our last hope to return to ourancient ancestral home. But these days are evil, and our hope is waningbefore this threat. Our strength has run out like water from a brokenpot, and although my ancestors would be shamed to ask foraid, I believeonly an outsider like yourself can save us now.

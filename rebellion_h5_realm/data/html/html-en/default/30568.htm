Urutu Chief Hatos:
Hestui, Atuba, Neruga, Gandi, Duda-Mara, andUrutu!!! Praise the great Orc tribes! I, the son of Haros, the shield ofUruchatte, and one of the 6 great heroes, remember! The stories of ourancestors passed on from fathers to their sons and to the sons of theirsons. ! Do not forget! The story that we ruled the world and broughtothers to their knees with our power. Do not forget! The story of ourburned cities and our people who were slain with the spears and swordsof our enemies.

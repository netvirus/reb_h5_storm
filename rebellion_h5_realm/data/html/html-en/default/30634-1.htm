High Summoner Galatea:
Upper level elementals have the ability toconverse intelligently with Humans, and are the ruling class of theelementals. The Magicians of the Ivory Tower have defined the hierarchyof elementals into Turen, Tilpo, and Liebe. The elementals that haveappeared in Gludio are the Flame Salamander, Earth Worm, and WaterUndine. Originally, these elementals could only be seen in hot magma,high skies, the vast desert, or deep sea waters, but now they arehere. It is noteworthy indeed that they have appeared in Human lands.

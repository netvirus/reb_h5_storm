Khavatari Aren:

The strong body and combat might of the Orc warriors cannot be obtained without effort. You can gain these only through the most difficult training imaginable: Kabart, tre drill of eternity. Those who achieve the combat skills of Kabart will earn the title of Monk.
Those who master everything about the higher art of Kabart, the motion of the body and its relation to the universe, will be granted the title of Tyrant. There is another rank higher than even this, but it has been sealed for a long time and is no longer granted. Its powers has been long forgotten.

Newbie Helper:
*A novice character* is one who is new to the world ofLineage II. Certain benefits are available to novicecharacters up to level 62. Taxes and other charges have led to a steep increase inprices for armor and weaponry. To help offset thisproblem, *novice characters can exchange their used gearfor full credit towards the purchase of new equipment* inany weapon or armor shop. Also, the threat of monsters may be overwhelming fornovice characters. I help them as much as possible..
*Come and see me for support magic that is appropriatefor your level.* This will help build the confidence you'll need to challengethe powerful monsters in this world.
Thirdly, *Soulshots and Spiritshot for Novices will be givenin addition.* A novice character can receive additional Soulshots andSpiritshot for novices if he completes a particular quest. The information for this quest will be provided later. And lastly, occasionally players find that they wish tochange the race or class of their character. *Novicecharacter benefits may be passed to a new character upto level 6.* However, once a character reaches level 6,then only that character receives benefits as a novice.

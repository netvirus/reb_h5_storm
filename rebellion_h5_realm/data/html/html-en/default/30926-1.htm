Priestess Restina:
The Elves of Innadril originally came here from theElven Forest around ED. 1200. Tetrarch Terestien and his young followerssettled in the Emerald Forest near Innadril Lake. This was the beginningof the Elven migration to Innadril. Since our peoples share a devotionto the goddess we have always gotten along well. Over time intermarriagebetween the races has given most of the people of this area some Elvenancestry. This has enhanced both their beauty and lifespan.

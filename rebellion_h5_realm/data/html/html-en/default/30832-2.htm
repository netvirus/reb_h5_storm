There are a lot of people with prejudices about necromancers. Humansare instinctively afraid of blood. And that seems to be the reason thatnecromancers are misunderstood in a variety of ways. If you areinterested in necromancers or necromancy, you may be able to find theway to it here. At least, I hope so.

Atuba Chief Varkees:
Tejakar Oroka! The Orcs have thousands of tribes!However, only seven tribes carry on the noble blood! I am going to singtheir names, so listen well! The brave Hestui! The cold-hearted Atuba!The persistent Neruga! The strong-willed Urutu, the resourcefulDuda-Mara! And the merciless Gandi! One more thing! There are tribes oftraitors that betrayed their own kind! Their names are forgotten!

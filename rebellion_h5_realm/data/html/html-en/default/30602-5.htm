You can begin to learn skills when you reach level 5. You will recieveinstruction in new skills at the Warrior Guild, which is the room on theright when you enter the House of the King, the biggest building in thevillage, right in the center of town. The prefects there will teach youyour skills. Now, pay attention; you'll need *SP* to gain your skills. SP is short for skill points", and they're earned along with theexperience whenever you defeat a monster. SP are deducted whenever youlearn a new skill. You can check your SP by clicking the CharacterStatus button, then on the Status tab in the window. Of course, thenumber of SP you need will vary, depending on the skill you're after. Ifyou use your head while hunting, you should have about 60 SP by the timeyou reach level 5. The prefects can tell you the skills in which you cantrain. Skills are divided into active skills and passive skills. *Activeskills* require that you intentionally use them, while passive skillsincrease your basic abilities from the moment you learn them. At level5, you'll want to learn active offensive skills first. Power Strike isfor those who fight with a sword, Iron Fist for hand-to-hand Fighters.

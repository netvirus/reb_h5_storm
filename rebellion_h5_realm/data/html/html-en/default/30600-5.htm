You may begin to learn skills when you reach level 5. Training inskills may be obtained at teh Temple of Shilen, where the masters andelders stay. The Temple is the largest building in the village, north ofthe village square. The masters there will teach you. First, however,you will need to acquire *SP* before you can obtain skills, SP standsfor skill points, and like experience, they are earned by defeatingmonsters. SP are deducted when you learn a new skill. If you are curiousabout you SP, click the Character Status button and view the Status tabon the window. You must expand SP for each individual skill you acquire.

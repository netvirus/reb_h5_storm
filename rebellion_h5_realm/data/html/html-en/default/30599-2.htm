Newbie Guide:
Consider where you wish to finish, and that will tell youwhere to start. The Kingdom of Aden is in chaos, because they are at warwith Elmore, and with Gracia across the sea. What better time will therebe to restore the Elven people to their ancient greatness? For now, themost important thing is to simply hone your skills in combat. What couldbe a greater priority in this time of war? Begin your training byhunting *red keltirs* or *wolves* near the village. Be cautious,particularly with the wolves, for they are very savage. When you find anitem after slaying a monster, try to equip any armour or weapon, and seeif it's better than what you have now. For the rest, if you have noother use for it, sell it at the stores, so you will have adena when youneed it.

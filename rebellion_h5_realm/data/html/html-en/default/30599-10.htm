*Nerupa* , who lives under the Mother Tree in the north end of thevillage, will usually have some small task for you. You should befriendher, for she will usually be of help to you. By all means, stop to seeher whenever you can, and perform whatever errands she may ask. Near thesouthwestern gate, *Pixy Murika* is seeking help. She worries that theforest has been unbalanced since keltirs and wolves began to multiply.

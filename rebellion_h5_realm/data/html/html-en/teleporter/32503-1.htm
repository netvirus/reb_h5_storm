Kratei's Cube Entrance Manager:
Kratei's Cube allows *25 warriors whoare level 70 and above to compete for 20 minutes* to see who is thestrongest. Warriors advance through the rooms of the Cube, encounteringhordes of monsters as they go. Defeat as many monsters and otherwarriors as possible within the time limit; whoever defeats the mostwins. If you are killed, you will be teleported back to the MatchManager after 10 secunds. If the match hasn't ended, you may re-enter. *Doing so will not decrease your experience score. * The 20-minutecompetitions start exactly on each hour and half-hour, and you cannormally register up until 3 minutes before they begin. (If there was nocompetition in the previous time slot, you can only register between20-27 minutes after the hour or 50-57 minutes after the hour)Registration and admission are impossible in the 3 minutes immediatelybefore a competition begins.

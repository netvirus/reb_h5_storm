Premium Account System
Information and benefits from Prmium Account System.
 

Once an account is made premium, every character on this account will benefit the bonuses the premium account has to offer. Having a premium account does not make you any more special than the rest of the players. You will still be treated like a regular player by the GM/Admin team.
Silver Account Bonuses
- Exp: +30%
- SP: +30%
- Can use command .whereis
Gold Account Bonuses
- Exp: +50%
- SP: +50%
- Adena: + 30%
- Can use command .whereis
- Access GMhop outside town.
Platinum Account Bonuses
- Exp: +100%
- SP: +100%
- Adena: +50%
- Can use command .whereis
- Access GMhop outside town.
- Infinity Shots/Arrows.
- Decrease global chat delay with 50%

Legend: .whereis (target name) - shows location + quest pointer of a friend, party member or clan member. Usage of GMshop outside of town only if the player is not at pvp, state of combat, dead, olympiad, events and other normal safe checks. Usage of Buffer outside of town only if the player buffs are below 2 minutes and its not at siege/epic/instance zone + gmshop restrictions. Infinity arrows/shots, you must have atleast 1 in your inventory. Show player statistics - ability to shift-click on players to view their common stats (Level, class, hp, mp, cp, pvp kills, pk kills, buffs).

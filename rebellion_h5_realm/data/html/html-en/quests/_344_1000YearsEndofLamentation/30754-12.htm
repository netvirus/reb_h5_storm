Antharas Watchman Gilmore:
My personal aide wore this key around hisneck. He stood by my side during the battle with Antharas 1,000 yearsago. He was the first-born son in his family. This key was veryimportant to him. I believe one of his descendants is living in *GiranCastle Town*. He has the family name of *Rodemai*. I am bound to thisvalley so cannot go myself. Would you please take the key to Rodemai inGiran Castle Town? I will pay you for your trouble.

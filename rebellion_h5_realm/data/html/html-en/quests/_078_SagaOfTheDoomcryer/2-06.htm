Duda-Mara Totem Spirit:
Take this stone that the god of fire himselfentrusted to me! It contains many powers. The greatest of all is thepower to recover forgotten memories from the era of heroes. Thememories are stored in Tablets of Vision, distributed throughout theworld. You'll find the first just south of the Tower of Insolence. When you've communed with all the stone tablets, you'll know whether ornot you'll endure the coming winter.

Maestro Reorin:
That is all very hazy to me. I was sitting on my frontporch, drunk as usual, and she appeared. She was accompanied by a blackknight. Although I shall never forget that day, I cannot tell you muchabout their appearance. Now it seems like it was a dream. I doremember that she had an other-wordly beauty, with blonde hair and blueeyes. The knight was quite old, with gray hair and a dark countenance. Other than that. I cannot say.

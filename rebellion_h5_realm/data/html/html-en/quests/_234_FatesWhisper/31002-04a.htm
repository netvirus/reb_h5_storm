Ah yes, I shall never forget her words. In a soft andmelodious voice she said, "Soon you will be visited by a very importantperson. One who will re-shape the face of the continent. Please make atop quality weapon for him. " Then she smiled and turned away. The blackknight then gave me a pouch filled with gold coins and told me to use itto buy the weapon. But then a few weeks passed with a visitor, and Ibegan to believe that she was just a demented aristocrat. I soon drankup all the money.

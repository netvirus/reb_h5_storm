Sentry Nelsya:
In a few days the *mass of darkness* dedicated toShillien and Gran Kain will be held. *Shillien's Hunt* will be held forten days before the mass. The purpose of this ceremony is to hunt wildanimals in the name of the goddess and offer them as a sacrifice at thesanctuary. Of course, the act of offering a sacrifice is only symbolic-- in the past, the hunt's purpose was also to acquire supplies forthewinter during our long period of hiding in the darkness. The hunt hasalready begun! The youth of the village are in the woods and on thehunt, armed with swords and bows. The fervor is impressive -- the Humanswho witnessed it called it the "wild hunt.

In a few days the *mass of darkness* dedicated toShillien and Gran Kain will be held. *Shillien's Hunt* will be held forten days before the mass. The purpose of this ceremony is to hunt wildanimals in the name of the goddess and offer them as a sacrifice at thesanctuary. During thehunt, all the youth in the village, armed with swords and bows, go outinto the woods to hunt. The fervor is impressive -- the Humans whowitnessed it called it a "wild hunt".

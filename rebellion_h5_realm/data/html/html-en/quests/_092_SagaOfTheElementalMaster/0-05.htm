Grand Magister Arkenias:
There are no scrolls or books that contain thespell of ultimate summoning. That information has always been restrictedto the gods, far beyond the grasp of mere Elves. The secret issupposedly written on six ancient tablets, in the language of the gods. The gods put these tablets in very out-of-the-way places, and stationedformidable beasts nearby to guard them. If you can learn the secretsof the six tablets, you will pass the test. Luckily, there is someonewho can help you.

Trader Kunai:
The Duda-Mara tribe is not vain or extravagant, andmerely wishes for the health and well being of the Paagrio Lord with allour hearts. It's unbelievable, but they say that his healthy body hasrecently been seized by an illness. The elders of our tribe are veryconcerned, and offer him this mysterious medicine, *spider dust*, thatcleanses the body and mind of all impurities! We hunted the greatspiders that live high on the mountaintop and dried their legs in thesun, then finely ground them into a powder.

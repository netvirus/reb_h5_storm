[Monk Class Transfer]:
A Monk who reaches level 40 can proceed with the 2nd class transfer to become a
Tyrant. In order to become a Tyrant, the player must successfully complete three quests. The
Trial of the Challenger may be undertaken by a player of
level 35 or above. This quest begins by meeting with
Kash of Dion Castle Town. The
Testimony of Glory may be undertaken by a player of
level 37 or above. This quest begins by meeting with
Prefect Vokian of Giran Castle Town. Finally, the
Test of the Duelist may be undertaken by a player of
level 39 or above. This quest begins by meeting with
Duelist Kaien of Oren Castle Town. Return

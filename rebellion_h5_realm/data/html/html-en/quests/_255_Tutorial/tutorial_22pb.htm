In order to become a Phantom Summoner, the player must successfully complete three quests. The
Trial of the Scholar may be undertaken by a player of
level 35 or above. This quest begins by meeting with
Magister Mirien of Dion Castle Town. The
Testimony of Fate may be undertaken by a player of
level 37 or above. This quest begins by meeting with
Magister Kaira of Giran Castle Town. Finally, the
Test of the Summoner may be undertaken by a player of
level 39 or above. This quest begins by meeting with

[Orc Shaman Class Transfer]:
In order to become a Warcryer, the Orc Shaman must successfully complete three quests. The
Trial of the Pilgrim may be undertaken by a player of
level 35 or above. This quest begins by meeting with
Hermit Santiago who in a farmhouse near the Orc Barracks in Gludio. The
Testimony of Glory may be undertaken by a player of
level 37 or above. This quest begins by meeting with
Prefect Vokian of Giran Castle Town. Finally, the
Test of the War Spirit may be undertaken by a player of
level 39 or above. This quest begins by meeting with
Seer Somak of Dion Castle Town. Return

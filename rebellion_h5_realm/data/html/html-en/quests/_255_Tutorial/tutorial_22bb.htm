In order to become a Hawkeye, the Rogue must successfully complete three quests. The
Trial of the Seeker can only be undertaken by a player of
level 35 or above. This quest begins with a meeting with
Master Dufner of Giran Castle Town. The
Testimony of Trust can only be undertaken by a player of
level 37 or above. This quest begins with
High Priest Hollint of Oren Castle Town. Finally, the
Test of Sagittarius can be undertaken by a character of at least
level 39. This quest begins with

Lidia's Diary:
The Darkness of Abyss The starlight is reflected in his smile. He speaks of courage, but I don't know where or even who I am!
Can a crazy person have courage? Who knows? His laughter rings out,
and again the word, courage. The are other words, but they're in an otherworldly language I don't understand. The Dark Mage with his darn omile speaks softly to me, holding my hand in the dark night. Courage.
Courage. Courage. Nothing else matters. My ceremony is still going on! What brings you here so late?

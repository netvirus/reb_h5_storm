Captain Mouen:
Ascalon was my teacher when I was young. He used to be agood soldier, but he is too old these days, and often lets his mindastray. In other words, age has begun to tell on him. But to me he islike a father, and I can't oppose what he says. Nowadays he is caught inself delusion and keeps saying that he himself is the only one who cansave the kingdom from its enemies. That's why I worry sick about him.

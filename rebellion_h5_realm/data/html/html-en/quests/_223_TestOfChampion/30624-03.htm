!Veteran Ascalon:
Would you like to hear about my splendid past? When Iwas young I used to be hired as a soldier. Back in those days I wanderedaround freely and fought against the alien army and made love withforeign women. There were times when things seemed to be too harsh, buteven then, my blazing spirits as a Warrior never burned low. The flamingfriendship among comrades, struggles, fierce battles, love and romance,I experienced them all. We were true Warriors. But I am too old now. IfI find a talented young fellow who will succeed me, I would like toretire in peace.

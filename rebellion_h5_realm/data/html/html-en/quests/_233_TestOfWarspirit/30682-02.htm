Seer Pekiron:
The lizardman shamans understood the power hidden inTornar's body. So they ran off with the remains. Then afterwards, withthe bones, they made several fetishes. Those fetishes are still handeddown among the Leto lizardmen until this day! The Leto lizardmen formeda strong force in the southern part of Oren. I suppose the Leto chiefsor shamans who lead the group, have the amulet made from Tonar'sremains. Go ahead and take back the remains of our hero! Those bloodylizardmen, dared to split the bones into a skull, rib bones, backbone,arm bones and shin bones.

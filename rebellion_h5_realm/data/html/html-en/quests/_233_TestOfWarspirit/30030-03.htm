Kiruna and Gelhaas were 2 Warriors who fought bravelyagainst the army of the devil, Beleth. Their army won every battle, butthen one day, Gelhaas was killed by the evil spirits of Beleth. ImagineKiruna's sorrow when she heard the news that her lover was killed. A fewdays after the incident took place, it is said that she went to thebattlefield with her lips tightly sealed and won a great victory overthe army of Beleth. But unfortunately, in the final battle Kiruna waswounded deeply. She was removed to the shrine for remedy, but in the endshe died and was buried beside Gelhaas. Some say that she refused to betreated herself. Sometimes, the feelings which you call as a sign ofweak-mindedness, can be the source of powerfulness.

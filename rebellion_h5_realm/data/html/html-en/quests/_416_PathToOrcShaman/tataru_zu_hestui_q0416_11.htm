Tataru Zu Hestui:
The great Hestui Totem Spirit guards the very secretof the universe: the means through which we communicate with thespirits. The language of flame cannot be taught nor recorded on scrolls-- it can only be learned, as you have done, through fire and pain andblood. Your first step is complete. Now, if you wish to be able tocommand the spirits themselves, you must learn the *Names of the 99Spirits*. Go to *Gludin* in the Human land of Aden and show Seer Umosthis letter of recommendation. He will guide you to the next step.

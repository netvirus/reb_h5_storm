Tataru Zu Hestui:
The language of flame cannot be taught, only learned. Confused? Ha! If you wish to learn the language of flame, my youngfriend, you must summon the Hestui Totem Spirit. The spirit, who takesthe form of a Kasha Bear, symbolizes dignity and wisdom. Beforeattempting the summoning, however, you must first bring me a *Kasha BearPelt and Kasha Bladespider Husk*. You must also hunt the *ScarletSalamander* and obtain one of its *Fiery Eggs*.

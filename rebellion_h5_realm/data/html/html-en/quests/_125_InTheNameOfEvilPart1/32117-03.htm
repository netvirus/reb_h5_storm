Wallamai means a bowl filled with blood. Once the Lost Nestwas a sacred place for the Elroki. Young warriors proved themselves bytraveling there and completing a ceremonial rite-of-passage. But thenthe dinosaurs of the Lost Nest became so vicious that few of our youngmen returned, and those who did were half-past dead with the fear ofwhat they had seen. In our day, only one warrior has successfullypenetrated the Lost Nest and returned: Mushika. He brought out a metalfragment that he claimed was a sacred relic of our ancient past. But hewas the last Elrokian to enter the Lost Nest, and it cost him dearly. Idid not expect that the chief would entrust our fate to an outsider likeyourself, but perhaps he sees more than I do of this matter.

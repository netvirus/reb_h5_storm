Blacksmith Poitan:
The Book of Knowledge? Ha! That was sold long ago. Triff left it to cover his bar tab, but I didn't think he would pay itback so I sold it. It was a Sage who bought it. He was a very eagerbuyer and paid handsomely for it. If you want the book, talk to him. Ihave his name here, I wrote it in the account book. Ah! Here it is!. It was a month ago. Casian is his name. *Elder Casian*. Address. *The Wastelands*. I wonder what he is doing in a place like theWastelands. Anyway, if you want to try to talk him out of his preciousbook, he is there.

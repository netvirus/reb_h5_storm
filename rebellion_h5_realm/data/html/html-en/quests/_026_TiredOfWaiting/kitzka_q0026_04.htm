Will of Antharas is a command stone that Antharasimplanted into its mostly powerful subordinates, thus gaining controlover their movements. It is also a tool that maximizes the magic powersof Antharas's subordinates. If you were to combine it with a SealedBlood Crystal, you could obtain an item with tremendous magic power. Forinstance, by using it, you can resurrect multiple people simultaneously!Will of Antharas can be obtained by hunting the Emerald Horn, DustRider, Bleeding Fly, Black Dagger Wing, Shadow Summoner, Spike Slasher,and Muscle Bomber, which can be summoned from the Dragon Vortex inDragon Valley.

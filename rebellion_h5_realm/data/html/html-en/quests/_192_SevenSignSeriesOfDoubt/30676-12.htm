Warehouse Chief Croop:
Ach! If everyone was as warm-hearted as you, I'd not need contractors, eh? I'd just ask people wandering by to help, and keep md coin in my pocket.
But I'm no leech, y'hear. I'll pay your way fair and square and I'll nae hear a peep of protest from you. I'll make sure it's waiting for you in ths wemple of Einhasad by the time you get ts wigh Priest Hollint.
Take this letter, and get going now. I've a body to bring home.

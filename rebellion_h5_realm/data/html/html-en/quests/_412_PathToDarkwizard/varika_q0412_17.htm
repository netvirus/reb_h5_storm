Dark Wizards can control the power of nature and performpowerful magic. But you should be aware of the origin of power thatenables it. Long ago, our mother Shilen was insulted by Einhasad andbetrayed by the Elves. Since then, the origin of our power is darkness,like our mother Shilen. Dark power consists of four factors -- anger,despair, horror and lunacy. You can qualify to become a Dark Wizard onlyif you collect the four seeds that symbolize these four things.

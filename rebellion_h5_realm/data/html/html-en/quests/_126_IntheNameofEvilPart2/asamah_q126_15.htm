After great deliberation, the two gods made Shilen the goddessof water, a role they considered suitable for her many talents. Eva,however, was only given the task of composing poems and songs tocommemorate her siblings' rule. As the goddess of water, Shilen showedherself to be wise and far-seeing, which is why the other gods entrustedher with the governing of those powerful primitive creatures. Had it notbeen for her efforts, those creatures would have long ago escaped thisisland to wreak havoc on the world.

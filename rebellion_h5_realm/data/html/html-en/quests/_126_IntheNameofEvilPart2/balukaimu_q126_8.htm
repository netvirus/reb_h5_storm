Balu Kaimu:
From the midst of his storm of memories, Balu Kaimu hasfound the one that you will need most. It comes not from an Elrokian,but is actually a memory of the ax Gazkh about its owner. It seems thatGazkh frequently communicated spiritually with its owner. The memoryends with the Warrior's Song, an ancient Elrokian song of battle. Theending is the clearest part of the memory, so the song must have greatsignificance.

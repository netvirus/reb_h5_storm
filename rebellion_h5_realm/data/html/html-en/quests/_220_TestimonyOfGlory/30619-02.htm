Vuku Chief Driko:
For us, survival is the greatest virtue. I expectedthat there will come a day when this seal will be traded for the safetyof our tribe. We have many difficulties in this area where there areoften conflicts with Men. We are planning to go back to our base camps. We are moving to the eastern swamp area. But the reason we moved here inthe first place was because of the grotesque insect tribe which drove usout. *If you sweep them from our area, I will give you back the Scepterof Flame of yours*.

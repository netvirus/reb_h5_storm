Priest Adonius:
Thank you very much, brother of forest. Then I shalltell you what you can help us with. Right now, we are short of ink andpens which are used for making the copies. To make the ink, we need the*Spider Ichor*. You can extract them from the *spiders of CrumaMarshlands* . And for the pens we need the *Harpy's Down*. I would begrateful if you could bring *20 of them, each* . If you bring me all ofthese, I shall give you a copy of the book in return.

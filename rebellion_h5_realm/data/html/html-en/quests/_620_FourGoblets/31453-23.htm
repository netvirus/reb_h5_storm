Nameless Soul:
Entry to the sepulcher is only permitted at certaintimes. Only one party may enter at a time. All parties must have atleast four members. When the gate opens, all party members should hurryinside to avoid being locked out. Once one party enters, no others mayenter until the set time. All party members are expected to do you afavor, and they all must have a sepulcher pass. Once you satisfy theseconditions, you should have no trouble getting in. But be careful whatyou ask for.

Urutu Chief Hatos:
Young Shaman of the tribe, thou shall remember! Thestory that we ruled the world and brought others to their knees with ourpower. You must not forget! The story that our cities were on fire andour people were killed by our enemies' weapons. Thou, who will takethe Test of the Lord! If you wish to be acknowledged for thequalifications of the Lord by me, Hatos, you'd better be aware of thetrue meaning possessed in the totem which we, the strong-willed Urututribe serve.

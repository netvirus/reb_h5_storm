Dudamara Chief Takuna:
Tejakar Oroka! I am Takuna, chieftain of theDudamara tribe! I will punish betrayers by order of the Lord of Flame. Isee by your necklace that you must be a Shaman on the Examination of theLord! Since ancient times our tribe has been known as an 'intelligent'tribe because we have produced more Shamans and clever men than anyother tribe.

High Summoner Galatea:
In the thousands of years since the world wasyoung, when Elves and the the elementals of the woods made theiragreement and declared their allegiance, summoning magic has evolvedinto a high level art. Along with elemental spells that command fire andwind, summoning magic is one of the 3 traditional magical arts of theIvory Tower. Summoning magic is not an easy path. High level magic isrequired to communicate with creatures of the netherworld, enter into acontract with them, and bring them to your side. A very strong will isrequired to make the summoned creature obey your command. Many magicianshave lost their lives for lack of a strong will when summoning. Therefore, high level summoning spells must only be taught to thosewhose skills have been acknowledged. If you wish to follow the path ofthe Summoner, you must challenge and pass the 'Trial of the Summoner,'which tests your abilities.

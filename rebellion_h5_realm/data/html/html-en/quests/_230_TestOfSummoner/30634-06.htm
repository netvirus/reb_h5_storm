High Summoner Galatea:
The card is engraved with the Summoner's nameand unique mark. The tradition of the Arcana Card derives from the needfor Summoner's to exult in their victories. As an apprentice Summonermay not begin battle until a higher level Summoner has given you a card. *If you do not have an apprentice Arcana Card, the opponent will notaccept your challenge. * You must give your opponent an Arcana Card eachtime you lose battle, so it is suggested that you make 3 or 4 extracards. If you win, you are given your opponent's Arcana Card. It is easyto see who a Summoner has defeated by looking at his or her ArcanaCards.

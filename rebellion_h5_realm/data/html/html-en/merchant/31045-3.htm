Kitzka:
The Sealed Blood Crystal is Antharas's crystallized spilledblood. It has lost the original magic power of Antharas's blood, but ifyou combine it with Will of Antharas, you can obtain an item withtremendous magic power. If you use it, you can resurrect numerous peoplesimultaneously. This Sealed Blood Crystal can be obtained by hunting theEmerald Horn, Dust Rider, Bloody Fly, Black Dagger Wing, ShadowSummoner, Spike Slasher, and Muscle Bomber that can be summoned from theDragon Vortex in Dragon Valley.

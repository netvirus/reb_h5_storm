Grand Olympiad Manager:
When the games of the Olympiad end, the pointsof the Noblesse who participated are calculated. If your points exceed40, you can trade your points for Noblesse gate passes. Each Olympiadpoint may be exchanged for 1,000 Noblesse gate passes, up to 2000points. Also, for a limited time only, a Hero or anyone assigned tobecome a Hero may receive additional Noblesse gate passes equal to 100Olympiad points.

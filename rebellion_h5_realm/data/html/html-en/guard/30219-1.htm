Sentinel Veltress:
In the age of great disaster, our goddess was filledwith sadness at seeing so many deaths. They say her tears formed LakeIris. A long time ago, just once, I saw the lake nymphs dancing on theripples as if they were gliding. Now all the lake nymphs have left, butsometimes if you take a walk around Lake Iris you can hear the echoes oftheir singing.

Sentinel Knight Smallberry:
Sentinels are also called the guardians ofthe forest, or watchmen of the fresh green. We are fighters who haveguarded the Elven Forest from outside threats for hundreds of years. Theorcs of the Fog Mountains, greedy Humans trying to increase their land,and our dark Elven brothers of the north -- all have attacked this placemany times. Each time we defend ourselves, and they learn a harsh lessonabout underestimating the sentinels. Once there were many sentinels, notonly around the village, but also posted as lookouts on the boundariesof the forest. However, now there are less than one hundred sentinels,so we rely on the *guardians* to defend the forest beyond the village.

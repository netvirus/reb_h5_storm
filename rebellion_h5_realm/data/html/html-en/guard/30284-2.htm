Sentinel Knight Smallberry:
Guardians are guardian spirits of theforest. When the god Einhasad created the Elves from the leaves of theMother Tree Glade, she also created the guardians to protect the forestwhich would be the Elves' home. They are still loyal to their firstpurpose, and are very hostile to other races that invade the forest. Even Elves, if they injure their own kind, are dealt with ferociously.

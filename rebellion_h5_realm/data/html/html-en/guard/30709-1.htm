Guard Norton:
You will find the Valley of Dragons south of the village. To reach the entrance of the valley you must go by Hardin's Academy andgo west. The Valley of Dragons got its name from the legend that dragonscome to the valley to die. But there is one dragon whom death does notthreaten. He is the most feared green dragon Antaras. He has no fearalthough surrounded by the graves of his brethren. Many adventurers haveattempted to slay this dragon, and all have perished.

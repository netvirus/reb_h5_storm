If you follow this road west, you will come to a fork inthe road. If you turn to the southwest, you will pass the AbandonedCamp. Once, it was a camp for the Gracian armies, but it has beenabandoned for some time and is now the camp for the ol mahums. Thosenasty ol mahums were hired by Gracia as mercenaries, but they wereunable to return to their country and were left here to plunder for aliving. So you must be extra careful when you're traveling near there,or you may be ambushed by ol mahum looters.

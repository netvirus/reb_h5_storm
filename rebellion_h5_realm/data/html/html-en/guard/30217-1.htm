Sentinel Berry:
There was a time when I traveled here and there on thecontinent and saw many races and what the Humans call "cities" --massive forests of buildings made by Human hands. I was astonished bythe large size of the market in Giran, the splendor of Heiness theFloating City, and the overwhelming grandeur of Castle Aden. However,most Human villages are lifeless, cold places built by butchering theland. When I finished my travels and returned to the small, quietvillage upon the lake in the Elven Forest, I finally realized that thiswas the true home for all Elves.

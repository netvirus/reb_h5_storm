Sentinel Rayen:
May the wind of the forest lead you to peace. I am thesentinel Rayen. Our duty is to drive away those who try to attack ourforest and village. The Elven Forest is no doubt our home. However, wehave lived in the seclusion of this forest too long and I believe thatliving our lives isolated from the outside world has made us weak. Iadvise Elves who have learned the basics of sword and spell to go outbeyond the forest into the land of Humans and extend their knowledge. The first gateway would be *Gludio*, located to the south of the ElvenForest.

If you cross this bridge and travel along the roadsouthwest, you will pass the abandoned underground fortress and theNeutral Zone, and arrive at the place that the Humans call Gludio. Gludio is the largest region in the Human Kingdom of Aden. However, itwas attacked by another Human Kingdom called Gracia from overseas a fewyears ago, so parts of the region have burned down. Since history began,Humans have always enjoyed cruel wars in this way. The war has left manyruins in Gludio, which have become dangerous places haunted by theundead. Also, huge insects lurk in the vast wastelands in the south. Asit is, if you decide to go there, you must be fully prepared before youleave.

Guard Byron:
Have you ever seen a unicorn? It is a very dignified andexquisite creature. Unicorns and many other beautiful creatures live inthe Enchanted Valley. The scenery of the valley is quite bewitching andmysterious. There are certain plants that only grow there. Mage legendssay that fairies live in the deepest part of the valley, hidden there toavoid being hunted by Men. I pray they are never found.

Captain Bezique:
Gracia is a kingdom across the west sea. All tradewith Gracia has been interrupted since the war, but there was a timewhen scores of Gracian trading ships would frequent this harbor everyday. The Gracians were our friends, and no one imagined they couldbecome such bloodthirsty demons in such a short time. When all is saidand done, you cannot judge a person on outward manner alone.

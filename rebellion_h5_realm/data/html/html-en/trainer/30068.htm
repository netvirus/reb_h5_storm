Priest Shegfield:
Welcome to the Holy Temple of Einhasad. Like PriestessGlyvka, I was born and raised here in Dion Castle Town. At one time, Ithought this land was cursed. The places outside of the castle andvillage are all very frightful, you see. However, I gave my devotion toEinhasad and afterwards my mind was at peace. Now I think this all wasthe will of the gods.

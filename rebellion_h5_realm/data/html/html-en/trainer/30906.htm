Priest Ranton:
Have you heard the story of Devil's Isle? It is said thatthe ghost of the great pirate Zaken haunts that forlorn island to thisvery day. He foolishly sought immortality and got more than hebargained for. Ha! All who have gone there in search of his lost treasurehave perished,may Einhasad have mercy on their souls. This has causedgreat distress in our temple. Oh, well, I'm sure from the look in youreyes that my warnings are falling on deaf ears. You, like so manyothers, are called to that wicked place like moths to the flame! Heed mywords! A corpse shouldn't be prepared for burial at night. Under thebright of Einhasad, all unclean things are burnt to ashes and blow away.

Blacksmith Bronp:
Always happy to boast about our guild! While the otherraces were still throwing bamboo spears and swinging stone axes, ourancestors had mastered bronze tools and begun crafting steel weapons. Only the best craftsmen are allowed entry into the Black Anvil Guild. We, in turn, have fashioned mithril weapons and adamantite armor worn bykings and princes. But beyond that, we have been researching the ancientdevices of the Giants. Our fearsome Siege Golems and revoluntionaryairships are the results of those studies.

Sorceress Juria:
To the north of this village is a massive ravine. Thatplace is a gathering place for dragons such as worms or drakes; so itwas named the Dragon Valley. Many hunters desiring a worm's scales or adrake's claw rashly venture into that valley, only to lose their lives. That is because the green dragon Antharas has summoned malignant spiritsto protect its home. If you are thinking of going there, you must beespecially careful.
